## Key Principles

### 1. **Cooperative Economics**
> "The bus costs the same to run whether it carries 1 passenger or 16.
> By sharing the journey, everyone pays less."

### 2. **Transparent Pricing**
> "Show customers exactly how costs are calculated.
> No hidden fees, no profit extraction."

### 3. **Democratic Service Design**
> "If enough customers pledge to use a route, it gets created.
> Community demand drives service provision."

### 4. **Capacity Incentives**
> "The more people who book, the cheaper it gets for everyone.
> Encourage passengers to invite friends/colleagues."

### 5. **Fair Wages**
> "Drivers paid UK National Living Wage (Â£12.21/hour for 21+).
> No exploitation, sustainable employment."

## Integration with Route Proposals

When a customer creates a route proposal, the system:

1. **Calculates Costs**: Distance, duration â†’ total fixed cost
2. **Generates Fare Tiers**: Shows how fare drops with more passengers
3. **Sets Minimum Viable**: Typically 8 passengers for Â£3.00 fare
4. **Invites Matches**: Finds customers with similar travel patterns
5. **Tracks Pledges**: Real-time updates as more people commit
6. **Triggers Review**: When threshold met (e.g., 16 pledges)
7. **Admin Approves**: Sees full viability analysis with projections
8. **Route Created**: Converts proposal â†’ actual Section 22 bus route
9. **Notifies Customers**: "Your route is live! Book now at Â£1.34/trip"
